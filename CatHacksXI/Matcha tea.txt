Tt Matcha tea
Pp 2 minutes
Ct 5 minutes
Sv 1 cup
Cs Beverage
Kc 5
Ii 1/2 tsp matcha powder
Ii 1 cup water
Dd Sift 1/2 tsp matcha powder into a bowl or chawan
Dd Pour 2 ounces room temperature water into the bowl
Dd Whisk vigorously with a whisk or chasen
Dd Bring remaining water to a near boil
Dd Slowly pour hot water into the bowl
Dd Enjoy
Nn Whisk with an M shape until frothy
Nn Can sweeten with honey or sugar if desired
Nn Can replace hot water with warm milk for a matcha latte
